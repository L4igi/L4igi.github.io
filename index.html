About ME

Recently, I finished my Informatics Bachelor's Degree with a specification in Media Informatics at the University of Vienna.
For me one of the most fascinating parts of IT is learning to understand the inner workings of technology and software that surrounds us.
Software development represents a canvas of endless possibilities, constantly provididing new challenges and opportunities. It is never static, there is always something new to create or improve upon.

During the last four years, University ate up most of my time.
After my degree I took three months off to work on some smaller personal projects. The goal was to create something that, in the end, I am satisfied with. For me Game Development is one of the most fascinating and interesting parts in this field. It brings ideas to life in ways no other medium is able to.
